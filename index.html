Types of Fish

Discover the fascinating world beneath the waves

Introduction

Fish are a diverse group of aquatic animals that can be found in oceans, rivers, lakes, and streams around the world. There are over 34,000 known species, ranging from tiny gobies to massive whale sharks.

Popular Types of Fish

1. Clownfish

Clownfish are small, colorful fish found in warm waters of the Pacific and Indian Oceans. They are famous for their symbiotic relationship with sea anemones.

2. Betta Fish

Also known as Siamese fighting fish, Bettas are popular aquarium pets known for their bright colors and aggressive behavior.

3. Goldfish

Goldfish are one of the most common pet fish. They come in many shapes, colors, and sizes and are known for their peaceful nature.

4. Blue Tang

Known as "Dory" from the movie *Finding Nemo*, the blue tang is a vibrant species that lives in coral reefs.

Habitats

Fish can be found in a wide range of habitats including:

  • Coral reefs
  • Deep oceans
  • Freshwater lakes and rivers
  • Mangroves and estuaries
